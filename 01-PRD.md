# 01 - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name

AI Video Clipper Platform

## Product Vision

Deliver an AI-assisted platform that transforms long-form videos into
high-quality, platform-ready short clips for creators while supporting
both Desktop Windows and Web SaaS.

## Target Users

-   Content Creator
-   YouTuber
-   TikTok Creator

## Supported Platforms

-   Windows Desktop (Electron + React + TypeScript)
-   Web SaaS (Next.js)

## Business Model

-   Freemium
-   Subscription (increases daily rate limits)
-   Credit System

## Core Problems

-   Time-consuming editing
-   Difficult viral moment discovery
-   Manual subtitles
-   Manual reframing
-   Repetitive publishing workflow

## MVP Features

-   AI Viral Moment Detection
-   AI Clip Ranking
-   AI Auto Subtitle
-   AI Speaker Detection
-   AI Face Tracking
-   AI Auto Reframe
-   AI Silence Removal
-   AI Filler Word Removal
-   AI Background Noise Reduction
-   AI Background Music
-   AI Caption Animation
-   AI Emoji Generation
-   AI B-Roll Suggestion
-   AI Hook Generator
-   AI Thumbnail Generator
-   AI Title Generator
-   AI Description Generator
-   AI Hashtag Generator
-   AI Translation
-   AI Voice Dubbing
-   AI Voice Clone
-   Batch Processing
-   Timeline Editor
-   Manual Editing
-   Brand Kit
-   Cloud Rendering
-   Social Media Scheduler
-   YouTube Upload
-   TikTok Upload
-   Analytics Dashboard
